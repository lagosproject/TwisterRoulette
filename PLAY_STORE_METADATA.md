## 🎨 Google Play Feature Graphic (Gráfico de Funciones) Spec & Strategy

### 📏 Technical Specifications
* **Dimensions:** 1024 x 500 pixels
* **Format:** 24-bit PNG (no transparency) or high-quality JPEG
* **Max File Size:** 15 MB
* **Color Palette (Kinetic Neon Theme):**
  * **Primary Accent:** Electric Violet (`#7C2EFF`)
  * **Secondary Accent:** Neon Cyan (`#2EFFFF`)
  * **Background Dark:** Obsidian Charcoal (`#0F0F0F`)
  * **Text / Highlights:** High Contrast White (`#FFFFFF`) / Muted Grey (`#B0B0B0`)

---

### 👁️ Visual Layout & Composition (Optimized for Conversion)
1. **Background:** A deep Obsidian-to-Deep-Violet gradient overlaid with a subtle, glowing Neon Cyan dot grid. This evokes a modern, premium "party/game night" tech aesthetic and draws the eye to the center.
2. **Left-Side Branding (Safe Zone):**
   * **App Logo Icon:** A 100x100px rounded neon violet & cyan badge representing the automated spinner.
   * **Headline (App Name):** Prominent bold title in white sans-serif font (localized).
   * **Divider:** A 4px thick Neon Cyan accent line.
   * **Subtitle:** An SEO-optimized tagline highlighting the key benefit (localized).
3. **Right-Side Device Showcase (Safe Zone):**
   * A premium, 380px tall portrait phone mockup showing a high-fidelity screenshot of the app's **Active Spin Wheel / Result screen** (showing the neon colors: red, blue, green, yellow).
   * The mockup has rounded corners (16px radius) and an Electric Violet neon border, with a soft 14px drop shadow to stand out from the gradient.
4. **ASO Margin / Safe Zone Rules:**
   * All key visual elements (logo, text, device mockup) are kept at least 70px away from the edges to prevent cropping on smaller screens or cover-ups by Play Store UI overlays.

### 🚀 ASO Designer Strategy & Rationale
* **Direct Value Proposition:** The layout is split 60/40 between clear branding and a real-in-context device screenshot. This immediately tells the user *exactly* what the app looks like and does.
* **Psychological Trigger:** Using high-contrast neon colors on a dark background creates excitement and energy, associating the app with nightlife, parties, and active fun (matching the physical Twister board game spirit).
* **SEO Alignment:** The localized subtitles repeat key keywords (like "party spinner", "ruleta digital", "roulette numérique", "party") which Google's indexing bots crawl, improving store listing relevance.
